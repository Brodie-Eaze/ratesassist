# RatesAssist
### Recover under-collected rates. No upfront cost.

---

## The problem

Council rates departments systematically under-collect from properties whose land use has changed but whose rating classification has not — mining tenements on rural-rate, vacant land that's no longer vacant, undeclared improvements, post-settlement owner-name lag.

Officers know it's happening. They don't have the time, tools, or cross-source data to find it systematically.

---

## What RatesAssist does

We cross-reference your rating data against authoritative external registers — DMIRS mining tenements, Landgate cadastral, aerial imagery, ASIC, ATO ABN Lookup — and surface every property where the evidence suggests a different classification or owner record applies.

For each candidate, we generate a council-grade **evidence pack** containing:

- The property record
- The current and proposed classification, with the specific Local Government Act provision cited
- Authoritative external evidence (tenement record, aerial image, ABN data)
- Estimated annual rates uplift + arrears within statutory limit
- Draft reclassification notice + objection rights letter
- Audit trail of every data source used

Your rates team reviews and decides. We never make statutory determinations.

---

## What it costs you

**Nothing upfront.**

We charge **12% of net additional rates collected** as a result of corrections we surface, capped at 24 months from each correction date. If we find nothing, you pay nothing. If we find candidates that don't recover, you pay nothing.

You only pay on rates actually collected.

## Appendix — Frequently asked questions

**Q. Who decides whether a property gets reclassified?**
A. You do. We surface candidates with evidence; your rates team reviews and your council makes the statutory determination per the relevant Local Government Act.

**Q. What about ratepayer objections or appeals?**
A. Standard council process applies. Our success fee is paid only on rates *actually collected*, not on candidates flagged. Objected or overturned reclassifications cost you nothing.

**Q. What data leaves council systems?**
A. Only what's required to identify candidates. Sensitive fields (hardship grounds, pensioner status) are excluded. All processing is in AU. Full audit log available on demand.

**Q. What happens to data after the pilot?**
A. Your data remains your data. On termination, we provide a full export and securely delete our copies within 30 days. This is in the MoU.

**Q. Is this AI tool reliable enough for statutory work?**
A. The detection scoring is deterministic, not LLM-generated. The AI layer narrates and presents; statutory citations are verified against state legislation. Every claim has a source citation. Officers always review before any action.

**Q. We're already a TechOne customer — does this fit?**
A. Yes. RatesAssist sits alongside TechOne, reading via supported integration paths. No platform replacement, no migration.

**Q. What state are you set up for?**
A. WA at first, with NSW, QLD, VIC and SA in the pipeline. State-specific statutory templates are maintained per state.
